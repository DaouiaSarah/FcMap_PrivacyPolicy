Privacy Policy

FC Map App

Last Updated: November 2025

FC Map is an Android app that helps you find classrooms, labs, lecture halls, and navigate around the Faculty of Sciences at University of Algiers 1, also known as 'Fac Centrale'.

This Privacy Policy explains how the FC Map app collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

Location Data: The app may access your device’s location if you grant permission, solely to display your position on the map while using the app. Your location is not stored or shared.

No personal data such as name, email, or contacts is collected.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your location is used solely to:

  • Display your real-time position on the map while using the app
  • Help you navigate efficiently

3. Data Sharing

Your location data is not stored, not shared, and not used by third parties.

4. Permissions

The app requires:

  • Location Permission (approximate or precise)

This permission is optional but needed for full functionality.

5. Security

We do not store any personal information on servers. All data stays on your device.

6. Third-Party Services

We use Mapbox Maps SDK to display maps. Mapbox may collect diagnostic data according to their own privacy policy.

You can read it here: Mapbox Privacy Policy

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Any changes will appear on this page.
